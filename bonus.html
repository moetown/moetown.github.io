Publications & papers

Informality enablers in the remote collaboration space

Co-writer on a conference paper for the Nordic Ergonomic Societies annual conference in 2017. The paper concerned the work that had been done by me and my thesis partner for Semcon and Ericsson during 2016. We did our thesis work within the context of improving remote collaboration from a UX perspektive.

The paper included the framework we had developed  for acknoledging informality between meeting participants and serves as a tool when designing digital tools for remote meeting. Feel free to contact me for a copy!

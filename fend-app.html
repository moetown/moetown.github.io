FEND

Mobile application

Eductating youth about opioids

February - September, 2018

The idea behind FEND is to engage youth into learning about opioids through an app using gamification to reengage, spur and motivate the user into continious learning. This is by the user achieved through answering questions, watching videos and infographics from motivational personalities backed by research.

I designed 3 different iterations of the app which all had a similar look and feel inspired by the Vans Warped Tours styleguide of 2018. Programs I used was Sketch, Invision, Photoshop and Illustrator.

The engagement was measured through the Preventum platform to improve the experience continiously. Followingly, I designed the FEND website with a similar look and feel combined with social media material, appstore and print material to match the entire brand. Other tools used during my work with FEND was Wordpress, Shopify and Fabric.

Designing for B2B air charter

Aug 2019 - Aug 2021
During my time at Avinode we were a team of 4-5 UX designers, distributed across 3 different products, and 2 continents. I mainly worked with our core product called Avinode Marketplace.

Highlights of this generalist UX/UI role was the close contact with end users and the a heavy focus on UX research. I enjoyed working across the pond with our US office where my UX manager was located, and half of the product team.

Tools frequently used: Sketch, Figma, Miro, EnjoyHQ, Invision, Lookback, Google Analytics, Adobe Creative Suite, Jira and Confluence.

Avinodes native iOS app

Avinodes core features as a private aircraft marketplace exists in a native iOS app, as well as a web app. The native app is tailored to fit better for users on the fly, litterally, who needed to request aircraft for their end clients, or to be able to quickly respond to those request on the other end by the operators.

I was part of the team who improved on the native app, making sure the core features were implemented and found delightful for its target groups.

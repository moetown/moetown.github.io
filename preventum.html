Preventum

Web design & rebranding

The design work was done using Photoshop, Illustrator and Sketch.

Creating a modern look & feel

September - December, 2017

Preventum as a company started out as a product aimed to assists in educating and supporting intiatives specifically targeting the opioid chrisis in America. Preventum as a digital service is acting in the background of the campaigns that are runned on the iPug platform and is now taking shape as a CMS mobile application campaign builder.

My role was to work on the brand for Preventum and create the website, the logo and print material. Much of the UX at the time of working with the application was taken from my work with the Mesearchr application.

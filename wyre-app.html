WyRe

Mobile application concept

Supporting the Salesforce community

July - August, 2017

WyRe's goal is to create a app for the workforce within Salesforce, enabling the users to find work and consultants more easily. I was helping them creating a concept mockup of their ideal app.

The two user groups the app aids to assist, consultants and employers, were given a survey to answer regarding their needs and how an app could fulfil them for an optimal UX. The survey was then used as a tool whilst creating 4 iterations of wireframes and mockups for the actual app concept.

Tools used during the process was Google Surveys and Sketch.

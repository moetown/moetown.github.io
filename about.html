Hey! I'm Moa

Why I started designing? Well, I guess I never stopped from the moment I as a child drew with crayons, built forts and told crazy stories about big hairy monsters. I have always enjoyed and explored the creative space in all its forms.

May it be UX, drawing pictures, developing websites or designing games - I love the creativity and exploration that happens when a group of people come together with their different mindsets and capabilities.

From a UX perspective, the fun and fabulous lies in the study of how people actually use something and figure out why and how to make their everyday actions more engaging.

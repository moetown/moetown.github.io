This is Moa

Why I started designing? Well, I guess I never stopped from the moment I as a child drew with crayons, built forts and told crazy stories about big hairy monsters. I have always enjoyed and explored the creative space in all its forms. As I during my engineering studies worked as a salesrep in tech and with a love to create my own projects it was natural to me that there was an interplay between creating form and using it which was everchanging. From a UX perspective, the fun and fabulous lies in the study of how people actually use something and figuing out why and how to make their everyday actions more engaging and efficient.

RealYou

Mobile application & branding

The design work included creating the UX concept and graphics for the app, an app logo, and brand guidelines. The software I used was Sketch, Illustrator, and Photoshop.

Take your workouts to the next level

February 2018

RealYou is a branch of the company RealMovement, a company aiming to increase the movment in society by building community,  and encouraging callisthenic exercises and change of diet.

As a part of that they needed a simple app to log the workouts of their new particiants in the RealYou program. The app allows the users to do callisthenic workouts and log their progress, compete against themselves and others, and gain chances to win prizes as they collect points.

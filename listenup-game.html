Listen Up!

Graphic & game design

Boardgame design

2018-2019

A friend of mine had an idea for a board game ment to act as a conversation started for topics you'd not normally adress. He just didn't know where to start. His amazing initiative "The Kindness Collective" had been a great community for me and others, and I wanted to give back some of the generosity I had been shared through this group. I told him I'd design the game for him, pretty much free of charge, and today I can say that I'm soo happy to see what has come of that! He's produced over 1000 games and have countless community events and workshops around Australia. Design has the power to affect so many people in a positive way, and that is its true power.

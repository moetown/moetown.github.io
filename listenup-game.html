Listen Up!

Graphic game design for print

Boardgame design

2018-2019

The game "Listen up!" aims to act as a conversation starter around topics which brings people closer together and opens up to a deeper and more meaningful connection. As a way to bring strangers together, this was a very fun and engaging project to work on.

The game has a few different decks of topical questions which are asked between the participants as they move across the game board. The focus of the game is not so much about getting first to the end, as being open and listen to your fellow players.

I designed the games graphic content and the packaging box using Adobe Illustrator and Indesign. The game is now produced and sold across Australia..

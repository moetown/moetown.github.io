As a UX Designer for Walley by Norion Bank, my main purpose is to improve the after purchase experience for our end customers. During my time at Walley we've worked a lot with data and insight management, getting better at understanding what it is our customers need and how they want those needs met. We're now taking steps to better aid those needs, more to come about this.

We're currently four UX designers at the company, all located in different product teams. We collaborate together weekly, sometimes daily, to align on everything from brand to accessability. I work on everything across the product lifecycle and my role is therefor very broad, which I enjoy.

Tools frequently used: Sketch, Figma, Miro, EnjoyHQ, Invision, Lookback, Google Analytics, Adobe Creative Suite, Jira and Confluence.

Helicopter search

The helicopter search engine allows brokers to find helicopters within a radius of an airport, and request those for a certain leg or several legs of a trip. The main purpose of the initiative was to upgrade the experience of the helicopter search page from an old code base and provide UI/UX improvements to the overall workflow.

I was using our Sketch design system components to create cosistency with other parts of the product as well as creating new components for future use. The project was usability tested remotely with targeted users to provide feedback. The design work was done using Sketch, Abstract, Invision, Zoom and Google analytics.
